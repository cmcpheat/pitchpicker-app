App Instructions

Book a football pitch

Pitches are booked via the "Bookings" tab. Here you can select the type of pitch, the size, the number of players etc.

Book football equipment

PitchPicker users can book equipment via the "Bookings" tab. To view the list of available equipment, just expand the option which says "Book Equipment".

Find a Sports Facility

We have a list of available facilities listed on the "Find a Facility" tab. From here you can click on your desired location. This will show the facility's location via the Google Maps widget.

View your previous bookings

You can see a list of your previous bookings by going to the "My Account" tab and expanding the "Booking History" section. Previous bookings are listed with their unique Booking IDs. Please note: if you have never booked with us, you will not see anything in your "Booking History".

Sign out of your account

To sign out, go to the "My Account" tab and select "Sign Out" at the bottom of the page.

About PitchPicker

PitchPicker is a mobile web application which is compatible with all smartphone devices and operating systems. The PitchPicker mobile web application is designed to enable members of the public to plan and book local 5, 7 and 11-a-side football pitches and facilities throughout the city of Glasgow and the West of Scotland. PitchPicker allows users to find their nearest football facilities using geolocation and Google Maps API. Furthermore, PitchPicker users are able to book equipment for playing football and training, such as; footballs, cones, hurdles, poles and coloured bibs.

About Booking

To Book a football pitch (plus equipment), visit the "Bookings" tab to complete your booking form. Once submitted, you will receive a booking reference which will be stored in the "My Account" tab, in the "Booking History" section.

About Equipment

Equipment can be booked along with the football pitches. Equipment which can be booked: footballs, bibs, poles, cones and hurdles. A history of the equipment which has been booked is also stored in the "My Account" tab, in the "Booking History" section.

Benefits of PitchPicker

PitchPicker aims to promote football in the local community, and enable people of all shapes, sizes and ability, the opportunity access local facilities in order to compete in Scotland's national sport.

FAQs

How do I book a football pitch?

Pitches are booked via the "Bookings" tab. Here you can select the type of pitch, the size, the number of players etc.

Can I also book football equipment?

Yes! PitchPicker users can book equipment via the "Bookings" tab. To view the list of available equipment, just expand the option which says "Book Equipment".

What sports facilities are available?

We have a list of available facilities listed on the "Find a Facility" tab. From here you can click on your desired location. This will show the facility's contact details, as well as location via the Google Maps widget.

Can I see my previous bookings?

Yes. You can see a list of your previous bookings by going to the "My Account" tab and expanding the "Booking History" section. Previous bookings are listed with their unique Booking IDs. Please note: if you have never booked with us, you will not see anything in your "Booking History".
